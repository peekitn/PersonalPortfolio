Games

By Francisco Alexandre

Star Wars: The Old Republic

Star Wars™: The Old Republic™ is a free-to-play MMORPG that puts you at the center of your own saga. Play as a Jedi, Sith, Bounty Hunter, or one of many other iconic Star Wars classes in a galaxy far, far away, more than 3,000 years before the classic films.

I love this game; it has several classes for you to enjoy, and many missions. It's a Star Wars game at its core.

Resident Evil 7

Fear and isolation seep into the walls of an abandoned country house. "7" marks a new beginning for survival horror with the "Isolated View" of the visceral first-person perspective.

For me, one of the best horror games of all time. I've finished it several times and I love doing speedruns from time to time. Simply an insanely good game.

Resident Evil Revelations

The acclaimed horror title takes players back to the events that occurred between Resident Evil®4 and Resident Evil®5, revealing the truth about the T-Abyss virus. Resident Evil Revelations features Jill Valentine and Chris Redfield, fan-favorite characters from the series, along with their respective BSAA partners - Parker Luciani and Jessica Sherawat. The action begins aboard a supposedly abandoned cruise ship, the "Queen Zenobia," where terror lurks around every corner, before players head to the mainland and the devastated city of Terragrigia. With limited ammunition and weapons, you must fight for survival in the terror of Resident Evil Revelations.

A fantastic game, I finished it on Nintendo 3DS, PC, and Nintendo Switch. It has many characters, good enemies, and a very interesting plot. Easily one of the best games I've ever played.

God of War 2

Kratos continues his quest for revenge in God of War 2. After defeating Ares and achieving his vengeance, Kratos ascends to Olympus and becomes the new God of War. But his troubles are only beginning: humiliated and betrayed once again, the Ghost of Sparta discovers the true game of the gods, in which he is merely a pawn. Now they must pay.

I've lost count of how many times I've played it in my entire life; it's a game you never get tired of playing.

Pokemon Platinum

Pokémon Platinum follows a young Trainer exploring the Sinnoh region, collecting Gym Badges, and stopping Team Galactic’s plan to control legendary Pokémon. The story highlights Giratina and the mysterious Distortion World, adding new twists to the adventure.

The best game in the Pokémon franchise, I'm very passionate about this work of art. I always recommend this game.

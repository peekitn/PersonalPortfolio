Por que fazemos o que fazemos?

In his book, "Por que fazemos o que fazemos?", philosopher and writer Mario Sergio Cortella explores the main concerns regarding work. Divided into twenty chapters, he addresses issues such as the importance of having a life with purpose, motivation in difficult times, values, and loyalty to oneself and one's job.

It's a very good book for reflection; throughout the book you try to understand more about your life.

Vietnã – a guerrilha vista por dentro

This report, in addition to being a historical document about the national liberation struggles and socialist revolutions in the region during the 1960s and 1970s, represents a faithful description of how the people fought, depicting the life of the Vietnamese people in the liberated zones, with their self-organization in defense, education, culture, health, and food production.

A very good book, I bought it with high expectations and it totally exceeded them.

O Chamado do Monstro

Ever since Conor's mother fell ill, he's been having the same horrible nightmare. So, when a monster appears in his room, the boy isn't fazed. But their relationship won't be easy: the monster wants the truth—and Conor is afraid of that.

When I borrowed it from the library, I was delighted with the book. It's so well-written, I always recommend everyone to read it.

O escaravelho do diabo

In this suspenseful story, we are introduced to Alberto, determined to solve this mystery. His only clue is the fact that the red-haired victims receive a scarab beetle in the mail before being brutally murdered. Driven by the need to protect the city and prevent further deaths, Alberto embarks on a dangerous and mysterious investigation. He needs to uncover what is behind these sinister crimes and find the culprit before it's too late.

Think of a really good investigative book; I've reread it several times. I simply consider it one of the best works of all time..

Geopolítica - Coleção 150 Anos de Futebol

In "Football: Geopolitics", José Eduardo de Carvalho provides a detailed analysis of the sport that has sparked conflicts and also overcome wars, thus becoming an essential piece in the intricate game of world diplomacy and geopolitics. Beyond its extraordinary economic potential, football can also make a valuable contribution to world peace.

I love football and political topics, so this book combines the two and demonstrates how politics has always influenced football.

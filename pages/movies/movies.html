Movies

By Francisco Alexandre

Super 8

During the summer of 1979, a group of friends witness a train crash and investigate subsequent unexplained events in their small town.

I remember being fascinated by this movie as a child; I even watched it more than 7 times in the same day. It's a good movie with an alien plot.

Star Wars: Episode V - The Empire Strikes Back

After the Empire overpowers the Rebel Alliance, Luke Skywalker begins training with Jedi Master Yoda, while Darth Vader and bounty hunter Boba Fett pursue his friends across the galaxy.

I received this movie as a gift from my mother when I was a child. It's a great film and one of the best Star Wars movies.

Rogue One: A Star Wars Story

In a time of conflict, a group of unlikely heroes band together on a mission to steal the plans to the Death Star, the Empire's ultimate weapon of destruction.

The first time I saw this movie, I went crazy. It's the most Star Wars movie possible. With interesting characters and a well-crafted story.

Deus é Brasileiro

God is getting tired. So he looks for a saint to take his place for a while.

The film was a very pleasant surprise for me; it's a film I hold dear, and I believe it should be more recognized by Brazilians.

Serra Pelada

A journey to the biggest open mining of the contemporary age. It's 1978 and friends Juliano and Joaquim leave Rio de Janeiro in search of their dream of striking it rich. Like thousands of other men, both get to the Amazon Rainforest full of hope and illusion. But routine in the mines changes everything and the obsession of wealth and power destroys them. Juliano becomes a gangster. Joaquim leaves all his values behind. This is a story about golden fever, greed and violence, a great friendship and its end.

Another film that deserved more attention from Brazilians. I simply thought it was a fantastic film; if it were up to me, it would win the Oscar.
